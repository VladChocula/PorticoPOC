Create Your Passkey

Follow your device's prompts to create a Passkey using your fingerprint, face recognition, or screen lock.

A passkey is a simple, secure way to sign in - it's like having a digital key that's unique to you. Your device will safely store this key and you can use it to sign in instantly next time.

Extra secure: Unlike passwords, passkeys can't be stolen or guessed - they're protected by your device's security.

Great news: If you use iPhone/Mac or Android, your passkey will automatically sync across your devices through iCloud or Google.

Your account will be created as soon as you click "Create My Passkey", if you abandon the process you won't be able to login until you complete the recovery process.

Tip: Only create passkeys on devices you regularly use. You can always add more passkeys later for other devices.
